What is the background of Sashay4Solar?

Sashay4Solar is the 2nd initiative by a group composed of Zeeshan Bhatti and Hamza Foy. This duo, formally a trio with fellow partner Kirk Kimsey, previously organized a charity 5K race at Iroquois Park to raise $10,000 for Syrian refugees named Sweat4Syria. In keeping with the naming tradition, we are persevering to construct the first residential-based solar array farm west of 9th Street in Louisville - our current endeavor has been named Sashay4Solar.

What is the intention behind Sashay4Solar?

The intention behind this project is to provide economic relief for those residing in the West End through the providing of solar energy to reduce utility bills to a number of residents. We recognize the economic disadvantages historically inhibiting the financial security of many African Americans in this city and we intend for this project to be both a relief for beneficiaries & a pilot project to show that solar power can be a viable energy resource in Louisville.
